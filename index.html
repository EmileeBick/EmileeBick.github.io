Emilee and Co.

A family owned, produce company from Southern California

Only Organic, and Free Roaming Animals

We farm only the most fresh and organic fruits and vegetables, picked right from the ground to your doorstep. Our animals are free roaming and always in a good mood.

Founded in 2004, Emilee and Co. have always strived to be the greatest for our world's environement, and our customers' stomachs.

Winning countless awards, from the Produce Excellence Award; seven years in a row. To winning Food Service Award last year, in 2018.

What makes us different from other companies?
Our attitude.

Our workers aren't employees, they are our family. We look forward to coming into work every day, to see all of our workers' faces and to see them do what they love.
